Category

Synopsis

As part of this project, I focused on designing an LP cover for the artist Saint Mesa. The main inspiration for the visual comes from the unique blend of mystical elements, nature, and the artist's experimental music, which I translated into the creation of 3D assets and final graphic design.

Results

Final Cover Design.

Final back design was inspired by the pixel sorting effect used on the front, but it has been adapted to a form of stretching pixels and now also functions as a bar code.

Process

Overgrown empty husk.

The empty husk of a man was was sculpted and remeshed in Zbrush, every other part of the process was done using Blender.

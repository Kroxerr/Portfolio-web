Synopsis

The task of "GLORIA" branding commission was to create a label design for the new coffee liqueur product. My approach was to make the label pop out amongst all other alcoholic beverages and make it stand out. Sadly the client disappeared without giving any feedback and instead found another company to finish the design.

Final result

Final label design

This is the final design of the label where I decided to take it in this colorful direction. The first version was more muted and retro but the client never got to see any of them.

Logo

Logo construction

The logo took this condensed and tall visual to showcase the meaning of the word "Glory". It is using a custom letter design to create an unique visual flourish.

Previous iteration

Previous label design

This was the first iteration of the label design. The task was to also to design a logo with a torch and a coffee bean. I also created some color variations that I later scrapped and changed for the current visual.
